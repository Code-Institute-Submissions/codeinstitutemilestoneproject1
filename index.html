Welcome to Excalibur Fencing Club!


New to fencing or wanting to get back into the sport? Our club caters to fencers of all ages from beginners
to advanced students with different classes geared towards certain ages and levels of experience.
Our club teaches all weapons- foil, épée and sabre, with a dedicated coach for each weapon.
Your fencing journey starts here. Join us today!
